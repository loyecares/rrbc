Engineering at its peak

Many aspects of building construction services affect the architectural and structural plan of a building such as the vertical and horizontal routes for piping and outwork systems, the weights, sizes and location of major equipment and plant, energy efficiency strategies and so on. Therefore, building services designs are integrated into the overall building design from an early stage, especially on large building projects.

We improve innovative industrial solutions

Our mechanical and electrical services engineers work closely with the architects, eliminating interference and conflicts between building services installations and the ongoing building construction which causes delays, alterations, wastage of materials, dismantling etc. Our consistent cooperation creates orderliness, leading to time and cost effective operations on site.
